CARROT PUDDING (Gajar ka Halwa)

Description

Gajar ka halwa is a combination of nuts, milk, sugar, and ghee with grated carrot. It is a light nutritious dessert with less fat than many other typical sweet from the Indian subcontinent.Gajar ka halwa is a popular dessert all over India and often served at most festivals and occasions. The dish is popular among adults as well as children.

It is not as difficult to make in comparison to other Indian desserts and can be ready in no time.

Ingredients

  • 8 to 9 carrots medium or about 4 to 4.5 cups grated carrots
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 4 tablespoons Ghee
  • 10 to 12 tablespoons sugar or raw sugar or add as required
  • Â½ to 1 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 10 to 12 cashews chopped
  • 10 to 12 almonds sliced or chopped
  • 2 tablespoons golden raisins
  • 1 pinch saffron strands (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse and grate the carrots
  2. In a kadai, combine the grated carrots and milk
  3. On a low-medium flame, bring it to boil and them simmer until the carrots have absorbed the milk and is reduced
  4. While the mixture is simmering on a low flame, keep on stirring in between
    5. .
  5. When the milk has reduced mostly, add the ghee, sugar and powdered cardamom to the mixture
  6. keep stirring until the everything is well combined
  7. The sugar should be completely dissolved by the end and the carrots should start leaviing some ghee on top
  8. To finish off, add the cashews, almonds, saffron and raisins. Simmer the halwa till all the milk is evaporated.
  9. Serve the halwa hot or you may serve it cold
  10. Enjoy!
